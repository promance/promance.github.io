Why?

No one wants to hate their job

As a new culture of work emerges, boosted by the digital economy, large corporations have started to transform themselves in order to keep both attracting and motivating talents, who are key for their very near future.

Large transformation plans may experience some difficulties moving from strategy to operations, in such organizations.

Collaboration between operational teams and HR is no longer properly equipped as cycles are becoming shorter and shorter, generating sometimes frustration from both sides.

Besides new digital tools, employees are very demanding upon new working experiences over traditional management.

So… what?

We've just started working on it

We'll tell you all about it in the coming weeks.

In the meantime, we’re sharing our findings about the future of work in a compact weekly newsletter. It's in French for now, ping us if you'd like to see an English version of it.
